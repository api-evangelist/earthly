Federal compliance frameworks create dense, overlapping requirements that engineering teams must satisfy continuously. In conversations with organizations that ship software to the Department of War, the pattern is consistent: small platform teams, large compliance surface areas, and a widening gap between what's required and what's enforceable at scale.
